General questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration is not required! Everyone can use it freely

What is the identifier in the homepage for?

It is to identify individual budget data. So don't lose your identifier. A preconfigured testing budget data is set to 1.

What features are there?

There is adding function and update function for budgets. You can update your budget via Planner. Also you can add spendings to your budget to keep track of your spendings and budget left.

How is the data stored?

The data is stored via MongoDB and retrieved via internal APIs developed.

What are the frameworks used?

The frameworks involved are Flask Server, Bootstrap and AngularJS. These are great for rapid prototyping of products.
